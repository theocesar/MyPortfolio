During my time at university, I gained knowledge in a wide range of topics and technologies, which helped me develop a strong ability to quickly learn and adapt to new tools and concepts. Software Engineering Internship at Volvo Group [03/24 - 12/24]

During my internship, I worked as part of an agile team following the SCRUM framework, where I gained hands-on experience in maintaining and optimizing critical business operations. My main responsibility was to provide runtime support, ensuring the continuity and reliability of production line processes by analyzing JCL routines, COBOL programs, and mainframe reports in detail.
Besides that, I also facilitated interdepartmental communication by identifying and documenting the treatment of bus models within mainframe COBOL programs, enabling faster and more informed decision-making. Additionally, through targeted analysis, I was able to significantly reduce mainframe processing costs by identifying and deactivating unused JCL routines and reports.
To further enhance team efficiency, I developed automations using Power Automate and Python, which optimized repetitive tasks and streamlined access to critical information.
This experience was invaluable in honing my technical and problem-solving skills while giving me a deep understanding of legacy systems and their role in critical business operations.
